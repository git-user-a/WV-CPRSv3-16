Viewing General Patient Information You can view demographic information for the selected patient. This information is read-only and cannot be edited in Vitals. To view information for the selected patient:
  1. In the main Vitals window, click the patient name button in the top left navigation bar –or– select Patient Inquiry from the Reports menu.
The Patient Inquiry window opens, displaying general information for this patient including Address/Telephone, Eligibility, Inpatient Status, Future Appointments, Remarks, Date of Death, Insurance, and Service Connection/Rated Disability information.
  1. Click Print to send this information to a printer, or click Close to close this window.
